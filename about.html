Who we are

Kayesther Security Company limited is a security outfit incorporated uder the Companies and Allied Matters Act 1990 by the Coporate Affairs Commission of Nigeria as a Company Limited by Shares. This outfit was established to close the chasms in quality security services and in response to the declining state of security in the country with a string resolve to make a difference and assist clients to maintain their assests without stress or tension. Kayesther Security Company is licensed under the Nigeria Security and Civil Defense Corperation. Kayesther organizes training programs for various coporate organizations and individuals in our well-equiped training center. We also provide in-house training and refresher courses for staff of all classes of business and non-profit organizations. We undertake the sourcing , installation and maintenance of electronic Security gadgets to prospective clients.
